My Design Work

My journey within User Experience (UX) and Design has been documented into articles called case studies, which outline topics and problem spaces that I have engaged in.

Read through at your leisure of my processes, from researching topics to implementing final solutions.

Electric Stride - Exercise Mat

UX Design, UX Research, Rapid Ideation

An exercise mat for people with Parkinson’s to train their gait and stride length. This was created during the Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) Design Competition.

Design Leap

Service Design, Secondary Research

A Design Lab incubated project to create a mentorship program revolving around students interested in UX/Design, mentors, and other relevant stakeholders.

Formfactories Website Redesign

UX/UI Design, User Research, Prototyping

A website redesign to market Formfactories’ business as a reliable 3D-printing service in San Diego. This was also a client project within a design practicum course.

Mundo Gardens

UX Design, User Research, Prototyping

Collaboration with Mundo Gardens, a community garden based in National City, California, to help with their volunteer management.

Cinemunch - Ticketing Kiosk

UX/UI Design, User Research, Prototyping

Ordering food and movie tickets within a kiosk as an exploratory design concept in a prototyping college course.
