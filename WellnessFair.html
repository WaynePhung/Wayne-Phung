Wellness Fair 2018 Recap

Wellness Fair was an event focused on the seven dimensions of wellness, which was hosted by Housing Dining and Hospitality (HDH).

Overview

Project Type

This was a client project for Housing Dining and Hospitality (HDH).

Background and Objective

This was a recap video for Wellness, which was an event hosted on March 29th, 2018, hosted by the University of California - San Diego’s main campus organization, Housing Dining and Hospitality (HDH for short). HDH wanted a recap video made from Triton Television (TTV), a student film and media production organization that I was involved in. I was tasked to fulfill HDH’s request for the video.

My Role

I edited a soundtrack, managed the footage, made drafts of the video, and made changes to each draft of the video before handing off the final version to HDH.

Tools

Premiere Pro, After Effects

Core Team

This project was independently made by me, with some critique from student executive members at Triton Television (TTV), a student film and media production organization.

Affiliations

Triton Television

Process

Editing the Soundtrack

There were no restrictions to the music that I was allowed to use for the video since HDH mentioned that it would take care of copyright issues. A soundtrack that I used was a remix of the original song Jammin’ by Bob Marley. Its explosive opening instrumental along with its energetic tune and dynamic sequences seemed appropriate for the different booths, physical activities, and social interactions that were consistently apparent in the video clips that I looked through.

Visual Effects

I experimented with creating an audio spectrum on After Effects so that it would be responsive to the beat of the soundtrack.

I have also made a gradient background for the ending portion of the video using free-to-use stock footage. This would be used to showcase and thank the sponsors for the event.

Sponsors

I was also requested to showcase the following sponsors towards the end of the video. I chose as an appropriate background for showcasing the wellness fair logo and the sponsor logos. However, the resolution and quality of the sponsor logos given to me were quite variable, and some of them had colors that had too little contrast to the background, so I decided to make a subtle white background for some of them to improve legibility of the logos.

Phrasing "wellness"

I was able to play around with the word “wellness” as well as only using one word for each noticeable beat of the music to conjure different repetitive phrases. Different adjectives would be shown where as the words “wellness” and “made” would be repeated. The adjectives were chosen carefully so that subsequent video clips that belonged to the last word would be placed in that specific part of the video after the adjective.

Final Draft

Results and Reflection

The final video was handed off to HDH, which was published on their YouTube channel. HDH would return to pay TTV to make another video.

This event recap was one of the earlier videos that helped me learn the basics of translating marketing assets, colors, and feedback derived from the event and associated client into a narrative-rich video. It was fun learning a bit about making the audio spectrum from After Effects, which was significant in inspiring me to explore other possible visual effects for my future projects. As a former student member of Triton Television, I can only hope that TTV continue its relationship with HDH and that I can make videos that would also bridge connections between other future partners.

That's concludes the media project! Thank you. I really do appreciate you taking a bit of your time reading what I have produced and learned. I only hope that you have learned something new from this project.
