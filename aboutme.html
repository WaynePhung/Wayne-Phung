About Me

I’m a cognitive designer with sufficient background in research and prototyping. Within the different student communities during my former undergraduate years at University of California - San Diego, I’ve grown to appreciate how being inclusive and collaborative with people has the potential to really solve large, complex, systemic problems in the world or to inspire others to make content-driven videos with excellent narratives.
