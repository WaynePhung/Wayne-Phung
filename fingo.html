Fingo

Within my COGS 122 Course - Startup Studio, my design team and I were tasked to leverage existing digital technologies and develop an innovative service to any problem that we wanted to address. Within 5 weeks, my design team of developed such a service aimed towards connecting customers to woodworkers to better service wooden furniture.

Design Brief

Develop a service with a novel business concept that addresses a problem between humans and technology.

My Role

  • User Research

  • Competitive Analysis

  • Value Propositions

  • Wireframing

  • User Testing

  • Video Commercial

Team

Task

To use existing technologies to develop a service with a novel business model.
