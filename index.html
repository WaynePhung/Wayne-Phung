More About Me

Chinese American, native Californian.


Striving to empathize with the world, one project at a time.


I'm just about to graduate from University of California, San Diego with a B.S. in Cognitive Science under the Human-Computer Interaction specialization.


I respect digital technology and it's potential to solve contemporary problems, but I see the need to explore as many ideas within design as possible to find the most preferable solutions out there.


With a love to explore the traditional, the radical, and the undiscovered ideas that design has to offer, I'm ready to work with others to make work matter to others.
